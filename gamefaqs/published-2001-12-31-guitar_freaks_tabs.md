 Introduction
XXXXXXXXXXXXXX


I'll get straight to it: this is a collection of guitar tablature for Guitar
Freaks.  Have you ever picked up this game, loved it, and said, "wow, I wish
playing these songs was that easy on the real guitar"?  Well, it will be more
difficult, but now you can be a rock star just like the ones in the arcade!

If you play guitar(if you don't, you won't find anything useful in here), you're
probably familiar with tablature.  It's like easy-to-read sheet music
specifically for the guitar; go to www.olga.net or www.tabcrawler.com for more
information.

As with most tablature, I won't ramble on too much.  Right now, I have tabbed
out all the songs from Easy Mode(!). I will tab out more in the future as I
receive requests, to the best of my ability.  E-mail me at slodeth@iname.com to
make requests, corrections, or anything else.  Put "Guitar Freaks" in the
subject to ensure a reply.

Song: Cutie Pie            Difficulty: *                     Tempo: 105bpm


This is a fun, simple song in the game, but the solo is a bit more complicated
in real life.  It's no Jimi solo, or even a Silverchair solo, but it uses a few
techniques that you might not be used to.  Play it slow first, and you'll catch
on quickly.  Use a nice, clean sound for this song.
